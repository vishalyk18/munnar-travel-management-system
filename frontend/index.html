Explore Munnar in One Day

Experience the misty hills, tea gardens, and waterfalls.

7 Must-Visit Spots

1. Eravikulam National Park

Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, this park offers breathtaking views of rolling hills and lush grasslands.

2. Mattupetty Dam

Famous for its storage masonry dam and the beautiful lake, offering boat rides with stunning views.

3. Kolukkumalai Tea Estate

The world's highest tea plantation. Witness the majestic sunrise over the ghats.

4. Top Station

The highest point in Munnar, offering panoramic views of the Western Ghats.

5. Attukal Waterfalls

A scenic destination known for its cascading waterfalls and rolling hills.

6. Kundala Lake

Historical dam and lake, famous for Neela Kurinji flowers. Enjoy a pedal boat ride here.

7. Blossom Park

A beautiful park with vibrant flowers, underground meditation center, and adventure trails.
